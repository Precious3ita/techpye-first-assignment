There is this saying that people are wealth, I met a lot of good people with beautiful personality in the sport taekwondo, starting from my coach down to me senior teammates and my colleagues in the same rank. Normally I have love for sports, so the idea of going through university without sport did not sit well with me, so had no option but to haunt for a sport that will acknowledge my sportsmanship. That was the first time I tumble on the sport taekwondo which I participate in up till this day. The love for the sport grew with time due to the treatment I was given and the same interest we all shared as a team.
